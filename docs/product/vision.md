# Vision

## What Rhythm is
Rhythm is a desktop-first planning hub that transforms recurring obligations, annual projects, and external signals into actionable weekly plans.

## Who it is for
- Busy planners managing personal and team responsibilities
- People juggling repeated life/admin work and date-driven projects
- Operators who prefer high-context desktop workflows

## Core value proposition
Rhythm turns intent into scheduled work by generating real task/project instances and organizing them into a feasible week.

## What makes it different from a normal task app
- Treats recurring work as first-class planning inputs
- Supports annual project templates with due-date offsets (e.g., “8 weeks before”)
- Incorporates external context (calendar/email/plans) as planning signals
- Emphasizes weekly planning orchestration over simple checklists

## Why desktop-first
Desktop gives space for multi-pane planning: navigation, timeline, and detail context simultaneously. This reduces context switching and supports power-user workflows.
